Lola VanWinkle is an undergraduate student studying Creative Technology at Illinois State University. Not entirely missing from her studies, however, is her passion for theatre. Lola's history in theatre goes back 11 years, starting in the children's choir in Quincy Community Theatre's production of Joseph and the Amazing Technicolored Dreamcoat. She has taken a break from theatre for the first time since she was nine years old to focus on her studies at Illinois State. Lola would love to work with theaters all around the country, even the world, using her skills in creative technologies to enhance the theatrical experience for everybody involved.
